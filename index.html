Our Story

Our Journey To Luxury

Where We Are

Since 2012, Aadhaya Hardware has been at the forefront of premium architectural hardware manufacturing. Starting from a small workshop, we've grown into one of India's most trusted names in luxury door handles, locks, and fittings.

Today, our network spans across India with 500+ dealers and distributors. Our products grace luxury homes, commercial spaces, and architectural marvels across the country.

🏭
🚀

Where We're Going

Our vision is to become the global standard for luxury architectural hardware. We're investing in cutting-edge manufacturing, innovative designs, and sustainable practices.

We aim to reach 1000+ dealer partners and expand internationally. Every product we create is a step towards that vision — blending timeless elegance with modern engineering.

About Aadhaya

Built on Quality.
Trusted by Trade.

Aadhaya Hardware is a premium hardware fittings manufacturer based in Patel Nagar, Rajkot, Gujarat. We design and supply architectural hardware that meets the demands of modern residential and commercial interiors — from elegant cabinet handles to high-security mortise locks.

Our products are built for dealers, distributors, interior designers, architects, and premium project buyers who refuse to compromise on finish, quality, or design. Every fitting we manufacture reflects our commitment to precision engineering and refined aesthetics.

01
Premium Manufacturing
Direct from our Rajkot facility — no middlemen, consistent quality, competitive pricing for dealers and distributors.
02
Design-Driven Approach
Every product is designed with modern interiors in mind — clean lines, premium finishes, and architectural compatibility.
03
Business-First Support
We support our dealer and distributor network with fast dispatch, proper GST invoicing, and responsive service.
04
PAN India Supply
Supplying hardware fittings to dealers and contractors across India with reliable packaging and timely delivery.
